about

Dr Ryan Banks is a highly accomplished computer vision and AI Researcher with a strong background in developing innovative solutions for complex domain specific problems. With expertise in computer vision, machine learning, and deep learning, Dr Banks has contributed to cutting edge research and real world applications in medical imaging domains. His work focuses on leveraging and proposing novel AI technologies to enhance clinically significant medical image segmentation, object detection, and landmark detection tasks, making significant advancements in the field of medical AI.

With a background in computing for digital media from the University of Sussex, Dr Banks has strong foundations in computer science and digital media technologies. Subsequently, he attended the University of Surrey for his MSc and PhD, where he specialised in data science and AI for medical imaging. During his PhD, Dr Banks developed novel deep learning methodologies for dental imaging diagnostic tasks. His research focused on utilising deep learning techniques to optimise and enhance existing clinical diagnostic processes, with additional focus on anatomy-aware and hierarchy-aware models.

Dr Banks has a proven track record of successfully leading and collaborating on research projects, resulting in numerous datasets and publications in top conferences and journals. His experience working closely with esteemed researchers such as Professor H Lilian Tang, Dr. Tan Sui, Dr. Yunpeng Li and Dr. Xiongjie Chen, resulted in many successful publications, publically available datasets, and successful UKRI i4i funding of £1.6M leading to the spin-out company BeCertain.ai. With a passion for pushing the boundaries of computer vision that produce real world impact, Dr Banks is looking for the next research direction that will maximise societal benefit.
